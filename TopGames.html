Top 10 in 2023

Find the Top 10 open World video Games in 2023

Discover, And Enjoy

10-Genshine-impact:

In a world of elemental magic, a lone traveler searches for their lost twin. Journey across seven nations, meet unique companions, and fight epic battles. Genshin Impact - open-world adventure in a vibrant, magical land. ✨

9-Spider-Man:

In Marvel's Spider-Man 2, Peter Parker juggles the chaos of superhero life with growing pains. Balancing the webslinging with college exams tests his responsibility. A familiar foe resurfaces, Venom, pushing Peter and his mentor, Miles Morales, to their limits. Brace for electrifying battles, a web of personal challenges, and the ultimate question: can one Spider-Man stand against two threats? ️

8-Cyberpunk 2077:

Neon glitters, chrome gleams. V, a hustler in Night City's underbelly, chases dreams wired with danger. Biochip holds the key, gangs the shadows. Implants or oblivion? Cyberpunk 2077 - where humanity hacks the edge

7-The Last of Us:

In a world ravaged by plague, hardened Joel smuggles a girl immune to the infection. Ellie, his reluctant charge, becomes the flicker of hope in their brutal trek across America. Haunted by loss, shadowed by violence, their bond defies the world's decay, a fragile ember in the darkness. This is The Last of Us - a journey of love, loss, and survival where humanity's darkest hour reveals its strongest light.

6-Assassin's Creed Odyssey:

Greece whispers. You, a cursed Spartan, hunt vengeance and truth amongst gods, beasts, and betrayal. Odyssey awaits - carve your legend in sun-scorched islands and mythical battles. Assassin's Creed Odyssey - rewrite your fate. ️

5-Call of Duty War Zone:

Warzone beckons: Verdansk shimmers in the desert haze, Verdansk '84 pulsates with 80s neon. Drop in solo, squad up, or rumble in plunder. Battle royale royale reigns, from Gulag redemption to epic circle showdowns. Gear up, tac up, outsmart, and outgun. Warzone - where every drop's a fight, every fight's a story.

4-Ghost of Tsushima - Director's Cut:

Steel sings on windswept Tsushima. Jin Sakai, a disgraced samurai, rises from the ashes to defy a Mongol invasion. Master the katana, bend the wind to your will, and rewrite your honor in a breathtaking expansion. Explore new lands, forge unlikely alliances, and face the haunting echoes of the past. This is Ghost of Tsushima - Director's Cut, where whispers of legend become your battle cry.

3-The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt:

The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt Geralt of Rivia, monster hunter, seeks his daughter, Ciri. Amidst war and prophecy, he travels through a vast, open world, battling monsters, forging bonds, and uncovering ancient secrets. The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt is a sprawling adventure, where choices matter and consequences echo.

2-Dying Light 2:

Sunken city, overrun. Aiden, survivor with a past, chases a lost sister in a parkour playground built on crumbling concrete. Infected swarm beneath, Renegades rule above. Day's for supplies, night's for survival. This is Dying Light 2 - jump, climb, fight, stay human. ‍♀️

1-Red-Dead Redemption:

Westward winds whisper change. Outlaw Arthur Morgan rides a dying frontier, loyalty tugging against ambition. Guns roar, bonds smolder. His choice: carve a legend in Red Dead Redemption 2.
